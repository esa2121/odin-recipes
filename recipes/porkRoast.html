Pork Roast

Overall Time

  • Prep Time: 30 minutes
  • Cook Time: 8 hours
  • Total Time: 8 hours 30 minutes
  • Servings: 8

Description

This Slow Cooker Pork Roast With Vegetables Recipe is one of my favorite home-cooked comfort meals. The pork turns out so tender, and the vegetables are cooked to perfection! This is perfect for feeding just your family, hosting people, or needing a set-it-and-forget-it dinner! It warms the heart and the home, but it also makes the house smell amazing at the same time!

Ingredients

  • Pork Shoulder Roast
  • Potatoes
  • Baby Carrots
  • Red Onion
  • Minced Garlic
  • Johnnys Season All
  • Pink Himalayan Salt
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Vegetable Broth
  • Celery

Steps

  1. Spray your crockpot with a stick spray now I cooked this in an 8-quart Crockpot and used an 8lb Pork Shoulder Roast. For easy cleanup, you can use in the crockpot when making this.
  2. Chop up your potatoes, onions, celery, and carrots into smaller pieces.
  3. Start to pack the vegetables around the roast in the crockpot. With picky kids, I prefer to leave my celery and onions in bigger chunks for them to pick out.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, minced garlic, and seasonings.
  5. I used to make mine, but you can use canned vegetable broth. Heat your water if need and then add in your better-than-bullion base, and mix in the bowl until the base has dissolved all the way.
  6. Pour the broth mixture all over the pork shoulder roast and vegetables, you will want to have a good amount of broth.
  7. Turn your crockpot on low and allow it to cook for 6 to 8 hours.
